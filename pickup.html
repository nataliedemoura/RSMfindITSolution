Pick Up Your Item

If you have found a match for your lost item, here is how to claim it:

Pick Up Instructions:

  1. Contact our lost and found office at findit@umb.edu
  2. Visit Campus Center, Room 101 during office hours
  3. Bring your student ID or driver's license for verification
  4. Be prepared to describe the item in detail to verify ownership
